Product Engineering Intern

Credit Karma

May 2025 - August 2025 Charlotte, North Carolina

I worked on the credit cards team to improve the reliability of the rewards ranking framework, which determines how credit cards are surfaced across different rewards categories. I wrote SQL validation queries that compared manually entered third-party rewards data against expected ranking outputs, helping identify discrepancies that could cause incorrect card recommendations to appear to users. To make these issues easier to track, I built a dashboard that visualized ranking errors across dates, categories, and affected cards. I also created an automated script that sent daily Slack updates summarizing ranking changes and newly detected issues, giving engineers, partner success, and other stakeholders a shared view of data quality problems.
